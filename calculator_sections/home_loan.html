Home Loan


A Home loan is a secured loan product principally availed to finance a purchase/construction of a residential or commercial property.


  • The Home Loan is taken against the property/security to be bought.
  • The borrower can receive loan amount up to 85-90% of property value.
  • The loan is payable in the form of EMIs spread over a longer tenure.
